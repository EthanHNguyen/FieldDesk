# Synthetic Unit Travel SOP

This local SOP is synthetic and intended for development use only.

## TDY Packet Routing Expectations

- Confirm the mission purpose, destination, and travel dates.
- Verify the traveler roster matches the requested traveler count.
- Attach approval evidence from the commander or designated approving official.
- Include a funding memo or line of accounting before routing.
- Include per diem estimate and lodging requirement.
- Provide mission-specific rental vehicle justification when rental vehicles are requested.
- Route to a human reviewer before any official submission.
