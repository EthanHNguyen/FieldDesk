# Prior Successful TDY Packet

This synthetic example represents a previously accepted Demo Training Site training TDY
packet. It is used by FieldDesk as a reference for expected reviewer evidence.

## Included Evidence

- Training order with destination, dates, and purpose
- Roster matching the requested traveler count
- Approval email from commander or designated approving official
- Funding memo or line of accounting
- Per diem estimate for destination and dates
- Rental vehicle justification tied to mission movement needs
- Unit TDY checklist completed before routing

## Reviewer Notes

Reviewer accepted the packet because the traveler count matched across the
intent, roster, and training order. Funding evidence was present at initial
routing. Rental vehicles were justified by schedule constraints, lodging
distance, and no available unit transportation.
